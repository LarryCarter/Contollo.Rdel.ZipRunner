# Engineering Note: Add Engineering Intelligence

## Summary

We decided that RDEL/NCS needs a formal Engineering Intelligence layer to preserve important reasoning from AI collaboration.

## Problem

AI chat contains valuable engineering reasoning, but chat history is difficult to search, reconstruct, and reuse later.

## Final Decision

Add Engineering Intelligence as a first-class project memory subsystem.

## Reusable Lesson

If an AI-assisted task required reasoning, failed attempts, or a non-obvious fix, convert the conversation into durable engineering memory.
